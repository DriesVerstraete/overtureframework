\subsection{Class LineMapping}\index{line mapping}\index{Mapping!LineMapping}

 This mapping a line in one, two or three dimensions.
\begin{eqnarray*}
    \xv(r) &=& x_a + r(x_b-x_a)  \\
    \xv(r) &=& (x_a,y_a) + r(x_b-x_a,y_b-y_a)   \\
    \xv(r) &=& (x_a,y_a,z_a) + r(x_b-x_a,y_b-y_a,z_b-z_a)
\end{eqnarray*}
